template<Ownership W, MemSpace M>
struct celeritas::detail::VecgeomNavCollection< W, M >

Collection-like container for managing VecGeom navigation states.

Since reference and value all behave differently for host and device, we only declare the class, and provide specializations for each type. The specializations are also explicitly declared before their definitions, since many of the definitions may not be available depending on which compiler or which compilation phase is active.

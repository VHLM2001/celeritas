Detailed Description

Construction options for geant physics.

Note that not all Geant physics choices are implemented in Celeritas.

  • coulomb_scattering: enable a discrete Coulomb scattering process
  • rayleigh_scattering: enable the Rayleigh scattering process
  • eloss_fluctuation: enable universal energy fluctuations
  • lpm: apply relativistic corrections for select models
  • integral_approach: see PhysicsParamsOptions::disable_integral_xs
  • brems: Bremsstrahlung model selection
  • msc: Multiple scattering model selection
  • relaxation: Atomic relaxation selection
  • em_bins_per_decade: number of log-spaced energy bins per factor of 10
  • min_energy: lowest energy of any EM physics process
  • max_energy: highest energy of any EM physics process
  • linear_loss_limit: see PhysicsParamsOptions::linear_loss_limit

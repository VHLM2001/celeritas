Detailed Description

Data for a single inner node in a Bounding Interval Hierarchy.

Note that the LEFT bounding plane position is the far right boundary of the left side of the tree, and the RIGHT bounding plane position is the far left boundary of the right side of the tree. Since the halfspaces of created by the bounding planes may overlap, the LEFT bounding plane position could be either left or right of the RIGHT bounding plane position.

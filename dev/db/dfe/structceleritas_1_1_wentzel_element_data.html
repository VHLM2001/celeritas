Detailed Description

Per-element data used by the WentzelModel.

The matrix of coefficients used to approximate the ratio of the Mott to Rutherford cross sections was developed in T. Lijian, H. Quing and L. Zhengming, Radiat. Phys. Chem. 45 (1995), 235-245 and M. J. Boschini et al. arXiv:1111.4042
